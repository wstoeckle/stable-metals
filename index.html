Physical Art Interrogating the Essence of Wealth

We explore the permanence and impermanence of wealth—its tangible impact on our lives and the fluid nature of its value. Our work questions what it means to possess, to lose, and to reimagine wealth.

About Us

Stable Metals is an art collective devoted to creating physical installations and sculptures that challenge conventional ideas of wealth. Drawing inspiration from both historical narratives and contemporary critiques, our work is a dialogue between the material and the metaphysical.

Our Philosophy

In an era defined by rapid change, the notion of wealth is as fluid as the art we create. We interrogate the permanence of value and the ephemeral quality of material possessions. By transforming common symbols of wealth into thought-provoking art, we invite you to reconsider what it means to own and to endure.

Projects & Installations

Our exhibitions traverse the boundaries between economic theory, material culture, and artistic expression. Each installation is a physical exploration of abstract ideas, crafted with precision and an unwavering commitment to critical inquiry.
