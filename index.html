One Million Pennies Cannot Be Ignored

We create installations from the smallest units of currency—pennies most people throw away. Accumulated, they become monuments. Wealth at its most honest: heavy, copper, undeniable.

About

Stable Metals works exclusively with coins—primarily pennies. We accumulate them in quantities that force confrontation. A single penny is worthless. A million pennies is three tons of copper that must be reckoned with. Our installations ask: at what point does the negligible become undeniable?

Philosophy

Currency is a shared fiction. We agree a piece of paper or metal has value, and through that agreement, it does. But pennies occupy a strange space—too small to spend, too numerous to ignore, hovering between money and material. We explore that threshold where abstraction becomes physical reality. Where belief becomes weight.

Projects

Cathedral vaults filled floor-to-ceiling with copper. Installations that require forklifts. Work that smells like metal and makes your hands dirty. We're interested in the gap between seeing a number (1,000,000) and experiencing its physical reality. Most wealth exists as digits. Ours requires industrial storage.
